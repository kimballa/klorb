# Implementing a plan

Read `docs/plans/README-PLANS.md` in full and follow its instructions exactly — it covers
where plans live (`drafting/`, `ready/`, `archive/`), when you're allowed to implement one,
the architecture-review step required before implementation, and how to move a plan to
`archive/` once done. Do not act on a plan (or on `drafting/` contents) until you've read it.
